Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority - learn how we protect your data

Last Updated: January 2024

Our Privacy Commitment

At JPG to Small (jpgtosmall.com), we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our image compression service.

üîí Key Privacy Promise

Your images never leave your device. All image processing happens locally in your web browser. We do not upload, store, or have access to any of your images or files.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Images or Files: We do not upload, store, or access any images you process
  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Account Data: No registration or account creation required
  • File Content: We cannot see or access the content of your images
  • File Metadata: We do not collect EXIF data or other file metadata

How Our Service Works

JPG to Small uses client-side processing technology:

  1. Local Processing: When you upload an image, it is processed entirely within your web browser using HTML5 Canvas API
  2. No Server Upload: Your images are never transmitted to our servers or any external servers
  3. Temporary Memory: Images exist only in your browser's temporary memory during processing
  4. Immediate Deletion: When you close the browser tab or navigate away, all image data is automatically cleared

Information We May Collect

We may collect limited, non-personal information for service improvement:

Website Analytics

  • Page views and website usage statistics
  • Browser type and version (for compatibility)
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)
  • General geographic location (country/region level only)
  • Referral sources (which websites link to us)

Note: This data is aggregated and anonymous. We cannot identify individual users from this information.

Cookies and Local Storage

JPG to Small may use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand how visitors use our site (anonymous data only)
  • Local Storage: To remember your quality settings and preferences (stored locally on your device)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. This may affect some website functionality but will not prevent image compression.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Website Analytics: To understand site usage and improve our service
  • Content Delivery: To ensure fast loading times globally

These services may collect anonymous usage data but do not have access to your images or personal files.

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect our website and your privacy:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All communication with our website is encrypted
  • No Data Storage: Since we don't store your images, there's no risk of data breaches involving your files
  • Regular Updates: We keep our website and security measures up to date

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data or store your images, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. However, you have the right to:

  • Use our service without providing any personal information
  • Clear your browser's local storage and cookies at any time
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

JPG to Small does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since we don't collect personal information from any users, our service is safe for all ages to use.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through our contact page.
