Our Story

JPG to Small was created with a simple mission: to provide everyone with a fast, secure, and easy way to compress images without compromising quality. In today's digital world, image optimization is crucial for website performance, social media sharing, and efficient storage management.

We recognized that many existing image compression tools either require software installation, charge fees, or compromise user privacy by uploading files to servers. That's why we built JPG to Small as a completely browser-based solution that processes everything locally on your device.

Our Mission

We believe that powerful tools should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to democratize image optimization by providing professional-grade compression capabilities without barriers. Whether you're a web developer optimizing site performance, a blogger preparing images for publication, or someone who simply needs to reduce file sizes for email attachments, JPG to Small is here to help.

We're committed to maintaining JPG to Small as a free, privacy-focused service that puts users first. Your trust is our most valuable asset, and we work every day to earn and maintain it.

Technical Excellence

JPG to Small leverages cutting-edge web technologies including HTML5 Canvas API and modern JavaScript to deliver fast, reliable image compression. Our algorithms are continuously optimized to provide the best balance between file size reduction and image quality preservation.

We support files up to 30MB with best results for JPG and PNG formats. Other formats (WebP, GIF, SVG) are converted to optimized JPEG format for maximum compression efficiency and universal compatibility.
